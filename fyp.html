Case Study: Restaurant Management Solution

A Restaurant Management Solution with Admin Panel, Tablet App and Kitchen Screen.

Al-Fajar Family Restaurant

  • Industry: Food
  • Size: 15+
A Web Application which helps restaurant administrator to keep the track of Stock, Accounts, Orders and Customers Feedback. It will be able to generate reports of profit and loss. This system help customer to order and administrator to manage the record related to stock, accounts, employees and customers. This project confront all the problems which were face by the manual system and automate it through system.

Project Requirements

Project Overview

The project that is developed is Restaurant Management Solution. There are some problems that exist in old restaurant systems. They take order from the customer manually on a piece of paper and bulk orders on paper lead to confusions in the kitchen. The web-based application will run on the tablet. The customer selects the food from the menu provided to him and sends the order. The order from the customer tablet is sent automatically to admin and the kitchen. Admin can easily update and delete the menu items. Admin can calculate profit and loss and manage the stock easily.This solution is convenient, easy and effective thereby improving restaurant staff’s work performance besides providing quality of service and customer satisfaction. This process has addressed many hindrances in food ordering process and management of restaurants by lessening the time of customer and management for ordering of food.
